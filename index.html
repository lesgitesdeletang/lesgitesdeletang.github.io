What do we offer?

All our gites are easily accessible and are open all year in the village of Champdray on a plateau on the edge of the Ballons des Vosges National Park. In an exceptional location nestled in the landscape, you have a particularly healthy environment throughout the year. At the heart of an authentic late 18th century Vosgien farm the, tastefully renovated with passion and in perfect harmony with nature, come and enjoy the privacy and comfort to relax.
We offer 3 gites, The Forester, The Quarrier and The Weaver, all of which have WiFi.

The Forester

A very large Gite designed for 10 people and ground floor bedroom with wheelchair access this delightful and exclusive development will not leave you indifferent.

Accessed from the Entrance Hospitality Room the ground floor includes a corridor leading to a large 45m² living room / dining room and fully equipped open plan kitchen (an American fridge, multifunction oven: grill, convection oven, microwave, induction hob, dishwasher, espresso coffee maker, dishes and kitchen utensils). The warm colours, dark woods and soft lines make up a modern, open and relaxing environment. A leather sofa corner, LCD TV and DVD complete the facilities.

A bedroom suitable for guests with reduced mobility with its en-suite Italian shower adjoins the lounge. A large staircase leads towards 3 elegant and comfortable bedrooms. Each room has its own en-suite toilet and bathroom with shower or bath.

Outside a private terrace with garden furniture and a barbecue is available so that you can enjoy the view, the calm and the surrounding countryside, since you are surrounded by fields.

The Quarrier

Enter through a large door off of the Entrance Hospitality Room this gite is fully accessible to wheelchair users. The "open plan" kitchen /diner fully equipped with a round table and chairs, a cupboard, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, induction hob, an espresso coffee maker, dishes and kitchen utensils. From this is access to a comfortable lounge (flat screen TV, DVD, and Stereo system) with a wood stove.

The 2 bedrooms are spacious and luxurious, each equipped with a double bed (one of which may be converted into two single beds) two bedside tables and a wardrobe that will enhance your comfort during your stay. Each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with Italian shower, basin and toilet and the bath towels are provided.

Outside a private terrace with garden furniture and a barbecue is available so that you can enjoy the view, the calm and the surrounding countryside.

The Weaver

Enter this gite through a large door that opens to a "open plan" kitchen /diner fully equipped with a round table and chairs, a cupboard, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, induction hob, an espresso coffee maker, dishes and kitchen utensils. From this is access to a comfortable lounge (flat screen TV, DVD, and Stereo system) with a wood stove.

The 2 bedrooms are spacious and luxurious, each equipped with a double bed (one of which may be converted into two single beds) two bedside tables and a wardrobe that will enhance your comfort during your stay. Each bedroom has an en-suite bathroom with shower, basin and toilet and the bath towels are provided.
