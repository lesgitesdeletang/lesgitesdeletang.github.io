What do we offer?

All our gites are easily accessible and are open all year in the village of Champdray on a plateau on the edge of the Ballons des Vosges National Park.

The Forester

A very large Gite designed for 10 people and ground floor bedroom with wheelchair access this delightful and exclusive development will not leave you indifferent.

The Quarrier

Enter through a large door off of the Entrance Hospitality Room this gite is fully accessible to wheelchair users. The "open plan" kitchen /diner fully equipped with a round table and chairs, a cupboard, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, induction hob, an espresso coffee maker, dishes and kitchen utensils.

The Weaver

Enter this gite through a large door that opens to a "open plan" kitchen /diner fully equipped with a round table and chairs, a cupboard, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, induction hob, an espresso coffee maker, dishes and kitchen utensils.
